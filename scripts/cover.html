A five-year roadmap to build the dominant trilingual sheep nutrition platform across the Maghreb, France, and the Middle East.
This document defines the commercial architecture for OvinFormulation v1.0 across three user tiers — Students, Farmers, and Feed Mills — and details the subscription, marketplace, and partnership revenue streams that will drive the platform from initial launch to €5 million in annual recurring revenue by Year 5. It covers product strategy, pricing, go-to-market, financial projections, risk management, and the seed funding required to capture the underserved 12-million-farmer sheep industry in target markets.
